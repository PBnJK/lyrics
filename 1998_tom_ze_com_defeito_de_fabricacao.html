Com Defeito de Fabricação (1998)

Tom Zé

Published under David Byrne's Luaka Bop label, "Com Defeito de Fabricação" (Fabrication Defect) is a concept album about third worlders, their transformation into androids via economical exploitation, and, more importantly, the "defects"—dancing, curiosity, politicizing—that make them more than mere machines of capital.
